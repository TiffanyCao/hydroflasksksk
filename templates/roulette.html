The Basic Rules of Playing Roulette

Roulette starts with players making bets. The croupier (or dealer) throws a ball into the spinning roulette wheel. Players can still makes bets within the process. While the ball is rolling at the roulette wheel, the croupier/dealer announces: "No more bets." At that point players are not allowed making bets The ball lands on a number in the roulette wheel. If there are winners who bet the number, section or color, they will be rewarded according of their betting odds.

Types of Roulette Bets

The players buy in for chips (or bring chips to the table), then place their bets on any number or propositions:
  1. Inside bets are placed directly on one or more numbers.
  2. Outside bets are placed on certain propositions such as high-low, odd-even, red-black, first, second and third dozen, etc.
  3. There are other combinations of bets that can also be made.
